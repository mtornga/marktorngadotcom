Is your tax bill fair?

You and your neighbors receive the same services: police, fire, roads, schools. But you may be paying significantly different property taxes.

The Senior Property Tax Credit costs the county $30 million/year. This creates a deficit that leads to higher taxes or budget cuts for everyone else.

It's a reward for turning 62 at the expense of young families, pre-k tuitions, and essential services.

We can fix this. Mark Tornga for fairness in property taxes.
